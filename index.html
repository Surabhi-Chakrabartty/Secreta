"Past with bad memories can never be deleted but can be diluted"


Bonjour fellow internet visitor!! Secreta bids a warm welcome to you. However different and unique an individual of this world is, We all have one thing in common- one dark moment of embarassment or heartbreak or shame or regret or anguish (and the list of superlatives goes on). We believe that memories can be diluted, can be masked if shared with the world. This dark moment of our past that still haunts us like a nightmare, topples the domino of our mental peace can be relieved if it is set free. Only way to relieve yourself would be to free this bird that is trapped in your mind, in your soul. Thus, we at secreta request you to free all your memories, that would free yourself, mask off the bad vibe and also give us a teaching that could be implied on our lives. Send us your entries, do it annonymously, free yourself from that haunting secret, that will in a way free yourself from your past. A past that haunts till date.
